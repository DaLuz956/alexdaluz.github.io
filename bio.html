About Me

I’m Alex Da Luz — born and raised in Brownsville, Texas. A tactical thinker, an empathetic mentor, and someone who believes that clarity and service go hand-in-hand. I bring real-world grit to the tech world, combining hands-on experience with a systems mindset.

My background includes years in the insurance industry where I handled complex cases under pressure, later transitioning into IT and cybersecurity. I've led grassroots tech efforts, like founding Cyberscorp X, to support local communities with education and hardware repair.

My mission? To solve problems that matter — with tools, patience, and people.
